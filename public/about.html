KASPER Dance Crew is an all-inclusive student organization at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, specializing in K-pop and hip-hop dance.


Our passionate community brings together dancers of all backgrounds, creating dynamic performances and fostering a supportive environment for everyone. From covering the latest K-pop choreos to competing in regional competitions, we're dedicated to sharing our love for dance with the campus community.
